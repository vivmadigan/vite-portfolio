Who is Viv Madigan?

Hi! My name is Vivyan Madigan, and I'm currently studying .NET development at Nackademin in Stockholm. My program spans both backend and frontend development and will conclude in June 2026. Originally from Australia, I relocated to Stockholm in 2020, where I transitioned from a decade-long career in the film industry to pursuing my passion for technology and problem-solving.

My move to Sweden brought exciting new challenges, including learning Swedish, which I studied intensively at Komvux and Stockholm University. This adaptability and willingness to grow have fueled my journey into programming.
